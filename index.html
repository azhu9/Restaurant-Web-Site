About Us

D-Licious Dishes is one of the cleanest, homeliest, and freshest little Diners for dine-in, take-out or delivery, in Sayreville, NJ. We opened our doors in May of 2021 and have since become known as the Birthplace of the Legendary "Honey Garlic Wings"! We pride ourselves in cleaning, seasoning our chicken to the bone, then frying it up nice and hard, just like you like it! We're all about good food, good vibes and a good time, so if you're looking for either, stop by! We'd love to meet you in person, but if you can't make it, you can always avail our take-out, delivery and catering options. Hope to see you soon!
